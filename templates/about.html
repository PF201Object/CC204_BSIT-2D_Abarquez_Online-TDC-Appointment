About Our TDC Appointment System

We make booking your Theoretical Driving Course (TDC) hassle-free. Our system provides a streamlined, online process to secure your slot and prepare you for safe driving.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the first step in your driving journey. By offering a clean, reliable, and responsive online booking platform, we ensure that every future driver can easily access the required theoretical training without delays or confusion. We prioritize transparency and user experience.

How It Works

This system connects you directly with available TDC slots. Once you register and book, your request is sent immediately to the administrative team for verification and final approval. You can track your status, check payment information, and manage your booking—all from your personal dashboard.

Why Choose Online Booking?

  • ✔ **24/7 Access:** Book any time, day or night.
  • ✔ **Real-time Status:** Track your appointment status instantly.
  • ✔ **No Queues:** Avoid waiting in line; book from the comfort of your home.
  • ✔ **Paperless:** All details are digitally managed and secure.

User Guide: How to Book Properly

1. Register Your Account

Click on the **Register** link in the navigation bar. You must create an account to secure and manage your booking. Use a valid email address as this will be used for communication.

2. Book Your Appointment

Log in and navigate to the **Book Appointment** link. Fill out the form completely, including your contact number, preferred date, and time slot. Check the available dates carefully.

3. Check Your Status (Pending)

After booking, go to your **Dashboard** or **My Appointments**. Your initial status will be **Pending**. The administrator needs to review your request and confirm the slot availability.
